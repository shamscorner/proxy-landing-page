Frequently Asked Questions

What is a residential proxy?

  • A residential proxy is an IP that is given by an Internet Service Provider, such as the ones at home.

What is the difference between a data center and a residential proxy?

  • Residential proxies are slower but get banned less frequently.

What is the difference between sticky and rotating proxies?

  • Sticky proxies maintain the same IP.
  • Rotating change it's IP every time the page is refreshed.
